INSTRUCTION SET:

Landing screen simply pick either button; booking or management. 

|--BOOKING--|

booking page will take you to the form which is laid out linear so you start at the top and finish at the bottom. 

Enter all information required to standard formats, anything miss-entered will display under the title. 

Pick up time is a 24 Hour clock. I.E (9:03 PM is entered as 21:04)
click green submit button once finished. 

|--MANAGEMENT--|
	
admin page will take you to the management side of cabs Online. 

clicking view-requests is suggested if booking-numbers are unknown to the admin. 
This will display all requests that are unassigned within 2 hours of the current time. 

Enter the booking-number in the text-box below the title and above the buttons and click assign-driver to assign the driver. 

Any errors and success will be displayed below the tile.
